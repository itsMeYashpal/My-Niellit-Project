Disaster management - for when things go south.

It is hard to face any sort of emergency and disaster as they occur suddenly in our lives. But at the same time, we can certainly reduce their negative consequences through well thought out plans and strong preparations.

Disaster Management

Disaster management refers to the conservation of lives and property during natural or man-made disasters. Disaster management plans are multi-layered and are planned to address issues such as floods, hurricanes, fires, mass failure of utilities, rapid spread of disease and droughts.
